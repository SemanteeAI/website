Personal Data Protection

1. Data Controller

Semantee ("we," "us," "our") is responsible for processing personal data obtained within private projects with clients. We do not collect personal information through our website or other public channels.

2. Types of Personal Data Collected

We only process personal data provided directly by clients within the scope of private projects. This may include names, contact details, and other information relevant to the project.

3. Purpose of Data Processing

Personal data is used solely for fulfilling project-related obligations and delivering agreed-upon services. We do not use the data for marketing, analytics, or any other purpose.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

We process personal data based on contractual obligations between Semantee and its clients or based on explicit consent when necessary.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties unless required by law or explicitly agreed upon with the client.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for the duration of the project and any necessary post-project support, after which it is securely deleted or anonymized.

7. User Rights

Clients have the right to request access to, correction, deletion, or transfer of their personal data. They may also object to processing or withdraw consent where applicable.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
