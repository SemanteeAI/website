Privacy Policy

At Semantee, accessible from https:/www.semantee.ai, we fully respect the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Semantee and how we use it. If you have additional questions or require more information about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us. This Privacy Policy applies only to our online activities and is valid for visitors to our website with regards to the information that they shared and/or collect in Semantee. This policy is not applicable to any information collected offline or via channels other than this website.

Information we collect

We do not collect any information on our public website. We only use technical cookies, we do not use any analytics tools or contact forms. If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:
  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • If you provide us with your contact information and valid consent, to communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the website, and for marketing and promotional purposes
  • If you provide us with your contact information, to send you emails


    • Cookies and Web Beacons

    Semantee only uses "technical cookies". These cookies are strictly necessary for the proper functioning of our website, enabling basic functions; however, they are not anlaytical cookies.

    Currently, we do not use any third-party ad vendors on our website. Third-party ad servers or ad networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on Semantee, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs. These technologies are used to measure the effectiveness of their advertising campaigns and/or to personalize the advertising content that you see on websites that you visit.
    Note that Semantee has no access to or control over these cookies that are used by third-party advertisers.

    Third Party Privacy Policies

    Semantee's Privacy Policy does not apply to other advertisers or websites. Thus, we are advising you to consult the respective Privacy Policies of these third-party ad servers for more detailed information. It may include their practices and instructions about how to opt-out of certain options.

    Children's Information

    We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity. Semantee does not knowingly collect any Personal Identifiable Information from children under the age of 13.

    Changes to This Privacy Policy

    We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. These changes are effective immediately, after they are posted on this page.

    Contact Us

    If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
